Our Investment Philosophy

JSJ Canada is a family office with a 20-year history of investing in promising businesses. Our organization invests in small to mid-cap companies that are on the precipice of major growth and need strategic investment to facilitate it. Our investment criteria allows us to invest in companies with operations in Europe, Australia, and North America.

We provide access to funds whether thats in the form of seed money, bridge financing, or simply an injection of capital from an established organization that is actively seeking investment opportunities. The companies we invest in present strong action plans with clearly identified targets. They are led by strong management groups with sector expertise and a track record of successes.

We invest in public and imminently public companies. Our portfolio includes private placements, secondary offerings, and IPOs, as well as alternative funding structures.

A Values-Based Funding Model

JSJ Investments was built on the belief that robust relationships result in strong synergies and better returns for ourselves and the companies that we invest in. Our modern approach to capitalization leads us to work with like-minded companies that are forward-thinking and innovative.

Our portfolio of companies are spread over three continents, and we have spent decades investing in companies across a wide variety of industries. The team at JSJ Canada has the passion, expertise, and commitment to provide strategic support for companies ready to accelerate their growth.
