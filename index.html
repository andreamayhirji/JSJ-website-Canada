Our Investment Philosophy

JSJ Canada is a family office with a 20-year history of investing in promising businesses. Our organization invests in small to mid-cap companies that are on the precipice of major growth and need strategic investment to facilitate it. Our portfolio includes companies with operations in Europe, Australia, and North America.

We provide access to funds — whether that’s in the form of seed money, bridge financing, or simply an injection of capital — from an established organization that is actively seeking investment opportunities. The companies we invest in present strong action plans with clearly identified targets. They are led by strong management groups with sector expertise and a track record of successes.

We invest in public and imminently public companies. Our portfolio includes private placements, secondary offerings, and IPOs, as well as alternative funding structures.
